How to Play

Goal of the game

Form a winning hand of 14 tiles made of 4 sets (triplets or sequences) plus 1 pair. When your hand is complete, press the WIN! button.

Tile types

  • Number suits: Dots (‚óè), Bamboo (sticks), and Characters (Ëê¨) from 1‚Äì9.
  • Honors: Winds (East, South, West, North) and Dragons (Red, Green, White).

On your turn

  • You draw 1 tile automatically at the start of your turn.
  • Click a tile in your hand to discard it to the center area.
  • Your hand always has 13 tiles, and 14 only right after drawing.

Calls and Kongs

  • When an opponent discards a tile, you may get buttons for KONG / PUNG / CHOW.
  • KONG: Four of the same tile (you draw again after declaring a kong).
  • PUNG: Three of the same tile using the discard.
  • CHOW: A simple sequence using the discard (logic here is simplified).

Winning

  • The game checks if your hand can be split into 4 sets plus 1 pair.
  • If so, you can press WIN! to end the round and earn coins.
